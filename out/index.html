Mission Statement

Computing Club seeks to spread and facilitate a love of computer science and public service among students of Pope High School.

Activities

Computing Club offers a number of activites for members. The following are just a few examples:

  • Meet with likeminded peers to discuss computer hardware, software, and security.

  • Hone your programming skills by competing with your friends in Code Golf.

  • Learn about cybersecurity by participating in Capture-The-Flag events.

  • Refurbish computers for the World Computer Exchange, a volunteer organization that, among other things, donates computers loaded with educational content to schools in need across the world.
